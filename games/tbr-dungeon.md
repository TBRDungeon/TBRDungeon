You wake up wearing nothing but white boxer shorts covered in little red hearts. A snarky AI voice informs you that you've been selected to descend into the dungeon. Your weapon? Books. Your enemy? Your own indecision. Welcome to The TBR Dungeon.

## The Premise

Inspired by the world of Dungeon Crawler Carl, The TBR Dungeon drops you into a three-floor dungeon where every space is a reading encounter. Your player token — yes, the one in the boxer shorts — navigates 42 spaces across increasingly dangerous floors while the System AI delivers snarky commentary on your every decision.

> "Oh, you picked *that* book? Bold. Stupid, but bold."

The goal is simple: survive the dungeon and emerge with a reading list you'll actually want to tackle. The execution is anything but.

## How It Works

- **3 floors, 42 spaces** — descend deeper as the encounters get harder and the stakes get higher
- **7 space types** — Mob, Loot, Boss, Princess Donut, Safe Room, Achievement, and Stairwell, each with unique mechanics
- **14 unique bosses** — face The Hoarder, The Algorithm, The DNF Pile, The Inner Critic, and more, each designed to mess with your reading list
- **600+ cards** across multiple decks — no two runs play the same
- **3 difficulty levels** — Easy (4–8 books), Normal (6–10 books), Hard (8–16 books)
- **~20–30 minute sessions** — short enough to fit between chapters

## Features

- **AI-powered book recommendations** — choose Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini as your recommendation engine
- **Game history & book list export** — track your runs and export your curated reading lists
- **Midjourney-generated artwork** — every card, boss, and encounter brought to life
- **Procedural sound** — atmospheric audio that shifts with each floor
- **Desktop app** — available for Mac and Windows
