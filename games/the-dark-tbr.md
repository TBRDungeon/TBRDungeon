There are other worlds than these. You walk as a gunslinger along the Path of the Beam, and every trial you face shapes the reading list you carry to the Tower. The Roland Deschain AI Oracle walks beside you — weathered, watchful, offering counsel in the cadence of a world that has moved on.

## The Path of the Beam

Inspired by Stephen King's Dark Tower, The Dark TBR sends you across four Acts of a mythic journey spanning roughly 80 spaces. This is not a dungeon. This is a pilgrimage. Each space is a trial — a palaver, a door between worlds, a visit to the Todash darkness — and every trial adds weight to the reading list you carry.

> "You have not forgotten the face of your father. But have you forgotten what's on your shelf?"

The Oracle speaks rarely. When it does, you listen.

## How It Works

- **4 Acts, ~80 spaces** — a longer journey with a gunslinger's pace
- **8 space types** — Palaver, Ka, Door, Todash, Speaking Ring, Charyou Tree, Waystation, and Transition
- **220 cards across 5 decks** — Palaver, Ka, Door, Todash, and Tower, each with distinct flavor
- **The Horn of Eld** — lock 3 or more books to earn this achievement and prove your ka-tet is true
- **3 difficulty levels** — Ka-Tet (6–10 books), Gunslinger (8–14 books), Tower (12–18 books)
- **~30–45 minute sessions** — long enough to lose yourself in Mid-World

## Features

- **AI-powered book recommendations** — choose Claude, ChatGPT, or Gemini as your recommendation engine
- **Game history & book list export** — track your journeys and export your curated reading lists
- **Midjourney-generated artwork** — every card and encounter rendered in Dark Tower atmosphere
- **Procedural sound** — ambient audio that shifts across the Beam
- **Desktop app** — available for Mac and Windows
